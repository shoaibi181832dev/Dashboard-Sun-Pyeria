Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pyeria free to use?
Yes! Pyeria is completely free. All 100+ commands are available without any subscription.
How do I play music?
Join a voice channel and use /play <song name or URL>. Pyeria supports YouTube, SoundCloud and more!
What prefix does Pyeria use?
Default prefix is !. You can also use slash commands with /. Change prefix with /setprefix.
How do I get support?
Join our support server at dsc.gg/pyerox or use /support command.
Can I create playlists?
Yes! Use /createplaylist to create a playlist, /addsong to add songs, and /playplaylist to play it!
